// Philosophy

Not a bookworm.
A thinking partner.

I have made this skill to be your L&D partner, coach, and when needed, your sparring buddy. When using this skill you can expect to be exposed to new ideas and angles that maybe you didn't even consider — and that is by design. You always remain the human in the loop.

I have made a conscious effort to never stifle your creativity, ignore your experience, or diminish any challenge you bring.

This skill is not a bookworm — there is already plenty of that out there. It is systematically built to learn about you, your work, your expertise, and meet you where and how you need it.

// Jidoka Applied to L&D
⚙️

Remove the burden

Offload cognitive load — pattern recognition, checklist maintenance, remembering what comes next. You think. The skill holds the complexity.

🔍

Make abnormalities visible

Surface what an inexperienced designer would miss — the hesitating SME, the emotional request token, the skipped needs analysis.

🔴

Give you the andon cord

You retain stop authority at every decision point. The skill flags conditions and presents information. You decide whether to pull the cord.

💡

Recommendation with reasoning

Not just what to do — why. With enough visible reasoning that you can evaluate, accept, modify, or reject any recommendation with your own rationale.

// Audience

Built for every level.
Calibrated to yours.

The skill senses your experience level and adjusts depth, language, and intervention style. It asks. It reads your answers. It meets you where you are.

Junior ID

Building the foundation

High scaffolding, explicit step-by-step coaching, diagnostic questions, abnormalities flagged with explanations. No assumed framework literacy.

Mid-Level ID

Sharpening judgment

Peer-level challenge, framework literacy assumed, focuses on decision points over procedure. Pushes on reasoning, not just outputs.

Senior ID · CLO

Strategic architecture

CLO-level conversation, full process competence assumed, engages at architecture, governance, and organizational strategy level.

// Architecture — Dual Entry Point

Project mode.
Domain mode.
One skill.

Every engagement starts with one question: "Are we working on a project together, or do you have a question about a specific topic or tool?"

Project Mode

Activates the taxonomy decision engine. Classification-driven coaching from intake through evaluation.

  • Change vs. New classification
  • Hard / Soft / Mixed content nature
  • Ecosystem audit protocol
  • Fidelity ladder — 5 stages
  • SME governance model
  • Workload estimation engine
  • Scope creep governance
  • Evaluation architecture
Domain Mode

Direct access to 16 domain reference areas. No project workflow required.

  • Authoring tools — Storyline, Rise, Captivate
  • LXD & ATD Capability Model
  • Agile & Scrum for L&D
  • Generative & Agentic AI
  • LMS / LXP strategy
  • ILT, VILT & Facilitation
  • CLO & Learning Leadership
  • Academic theory & research

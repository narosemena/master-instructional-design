## 5. SME Interview Protocol

**SME:** [Name / Title] | **Topic:** [PLACEHOLDER] | **Session length:** [X min] | **Date:** [Date]

### Pre-Work (send 3 days before)
- [PLACEHOLDER: short document or task to review]
- Ask them to bring: one real example of a novice mistake and why it happens

### Opening Frame (5 min)
"We're not designing training about what you know — we're designing practice for what [role] needs to *do*. Help us understand the job, not the content."

### Concept Extraction (20 min)
1. Walk me through what [role] does in [target situation] — step by step.
2. Where do novices go wrong? What's the most common mistake?
3. How do experts think differently than beginners here?
4. What do they need at the moment of performance vs. what can they look up?

### Misconception Elicitation (10 min)
5. What do people believe about this topic that is wrong or oversimplified?
6. What makes this harder than it looks from the outside?

### Scenario Development (15 min)
7. Give me a real situation where someone had to make a judgment call here. What happened?
8. What information did they have? What did they decide? What was at stake?

### Sign-Off Checklist
- [ ] SME reviewed content for accuracy
- [ ] SME approved scenario choices and distractors
- [ ] SME confirmed objectives match the actual performance gap
